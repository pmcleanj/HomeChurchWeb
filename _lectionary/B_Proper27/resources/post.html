Collect (ECUSA):

Blessed Lord, who caused all holy Scriptures to be written for our learning: Grant us so to hear them, read, mark, learn, and inwardly digest them, that we may embrace and ever hold fast the blessed hope of everlasting life, which you have given us in our Savior Jesus Christ; who lives and reigns with you and the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever. Amen. (This collect is the traditional one for the second Sunday of Advent – note its more classical form in contrast to some of the more contemporary collects.)

Blessed Lord,
You gave us the Bible, for us to learn from.
Help us to listen when it is read, to read it for ourselves, to learn what it teaches, and to think deeply about those lessons.
Those lessons teach us to hope for everlasting life, given to us by Jesus
who lives and reigns with you and the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever. Amen.
