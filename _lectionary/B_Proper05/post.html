Collect (ECUSA):

Keep, O Lord, your household the Church in your steadfast faith and love, that through your grace we may proclaim your truth with boldness, and minister your justice with compassion; for the sake of our Savior Jesus Christ, who lives and reigns with you and the Holy Spirit, one God, now and for ever. Amen.

O Lord,
your Church is your family!
Help us to love you as our Father and to love one another like brothers and sisters.
Give us grace to tell others the truth about you.
Help us to be fair and kind to those around us
for the sake of our Savior Jesus Christ, who lives and reigns with you and the Holy Spirit, one God, now and for ever. Amen.
