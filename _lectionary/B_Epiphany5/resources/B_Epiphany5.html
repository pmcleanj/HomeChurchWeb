Lectionary Bible Study

9:15 to 10:15

Everyone* welcome!

*That means, everyone. Bring your baby or toddler or great-grandparent; come alone or come together; Teens welcome, Seniors just as welcome – every other age welcome too. We'll lend you a bible if you need one. We'll help you find your place and be patient while you find the right words to share your insights with us.

God has accepted us! God is patient with us!
The least we can do is accept and be patient with one another.

Open the door and come on in!
