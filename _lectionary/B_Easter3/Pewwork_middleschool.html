3But know that the LORD hath set apart

him that is godly for himself: the LORD will hear when I call unto him.
4Stand in awe, and sin not: commune with your own heart upon your bed, and be still. Selah.

6There be many that say, Who will shew

us any good? LORD, lift thou up the light of thy countenance upon us.

8I will both lay me down in peace, and sleep: for thou, LORD, only makest me dwell in safety.
