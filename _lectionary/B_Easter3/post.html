Collect (ECUSA):

O God, whose blessed Son made himself known to his disciples in the breaking of bread: Open the eyes of our faith, that we may behold him in all his redeeming work; who lives and reigns with you, in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, now and for ever. Amen.

O God,
The Disciples recognized Your Son Jesus when He broke bread with them.
Help us to recognize Him and His work,
everywhere where His work is being done in this world;
who lives and reigns with you, in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, now and for ever. Amen.
