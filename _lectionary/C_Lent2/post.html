Collect (ECUSA):

O God, whose glory it is always to have mercy: Be gracious to all who have gone astray from your ways, and bring them again with penitent hearts and steadfast faith to embrace and hold fast the unchangeable truth of your Word, Jesus Christ your Son; who with you and the Holy Spirit lives and reigns, one God, for ever and ever. Amen.

O God,
You are are glorious! You forgive us when we don't deserve it!
Help everyone who has failed to do what you require,
to be sorry and to return to You: to listen to You and follow You;
to know that Jesus Christ is your Son;
who with you and the Holy Spirit lives and reigns, one God, for ever and ever. Amen.
