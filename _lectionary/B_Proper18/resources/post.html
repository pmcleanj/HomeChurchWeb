Collect (a new experiment I'm trying for Sara – feedback? -- post your own vernacular translation of the collect?)

Grant us, Lord, not to be anxious about earthly things, but to love things heavenly; and even now, while we are placed among things that are passing away, to hold fast to those that shall endure; through Jesus Christ our Lord, who lives and reigns with you and the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever. Amen.

Help us, Lord,
to care less about things like toys and clothes and possessions,
and to care more about what You care about: love, peace, kindness, justice, and service.
Possessions can't last forever,
but what You value, does last forever.
Help us to value the things that last;
through Jesus Christ our Lord, who lives and reigns with you and the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever. Amen.
