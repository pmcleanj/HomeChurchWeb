Two exceptional declarations are made in this week’s Gospel text. How does it make you feel when Jesus says to you, “no longer do I call you my servant, you are my friend”? How does it make you feel when Jesus says, “you did not choose me but I chose you”? How might others feel if we made this declaration to them?

After his encounter with God, Peter’s approach to those who differed from him was marked by humility and openness. What does the story of Peter in Acts 10 have to teach us? Who are the “them” today that we consider to be “unclean”? How should we approach them in light of the week’s texts?
