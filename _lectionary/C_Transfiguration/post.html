O God,
You showed that Jesus was your Son
when his Glory shone out on the Mount of the Transfiguration.
Help us to know who Jesus is,
Help us to follow Him even when it is difficult,

through Jesus Christ our Lord, who lives and reigns with you and the Holy Spirit, one God, for ever and ever. Amen.
