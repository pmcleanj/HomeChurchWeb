Collect (ECUSA):

Set us free, O God, from the bondage of our sins, and give us the liberty of that abundant life which you have made known to us in your Son our Savior Jesus Christ; who lives and reigns with you, in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, now and for ever. Amen. Amen.

O God,
You sent us Jesus,
with the promise of “abundant life”.
Break us free from the sins that hold us back from our “abundant life”
through your Son our Savior Jesus Christ; who lives and reigns with you, in the unity of the Holy Spirit, one God, now and for ever. Amen.
