The Repose of St. Herman of Alaska
celebrated at St. Andrew Church
Mingo Junction, Ohio
December 13, 2003

 

Click on Thumbnail for Larger Version:

On Saturday, December 13, 2003, the Feast of the Repose of St. Herman of Alaska, Protopresbyter Robert Kondratick, OCA Chancellor, concelebrated the Liturgy at St. Andrew Church, Mingo Junction, OH.

Concelebrating the Liturgy with Fr. Kondratick was the V. Rev. Michael Zahirsky, rector.
The well-maintained parish cemetery is adjacent to St. Andrew Church.
A relic of St. Herman was available for veneration on the feast.
The interior of St. Andrew Church.

Mrs. Anna Mae Halischak directs the parish choir.

At the conclusion of the Liturgy, Fr. Kondratick presented an icon of St. Herman of Alaska to Fr. Zahirsky and the parish faithful on behalf of His Beatitude, Metropolitan HERMAN.
The faithful venerate the Holy Cross.
 
Icon prints of the North American Saints are distributed by altar servers.
