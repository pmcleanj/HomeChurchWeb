Review responses to last week's homework:

  1. What does our church building -- its art, architecture, and its use -- express your relationship with God?

  2. In what ways are you preparing for the coming of Christ?

  3. In what ways are you actively working to make God's Kingdom come?

  4. Who, in our time and place, might be falsely claiming “I am He” to try to gain followers?

  5. Does the inscription “This is the king of the Jews.” describe Jesus appropriately – why, or why not?

  1. Why would the religious leaders try to trap Jesus?

  2. How does this type of testing and questioning continue today – or does it?

  3. How would you respond to questions about Jesus that seem to be meant as a trap?

  4. What are things not to say?

  5. How would your answers differ if the questions seemed sincere?
