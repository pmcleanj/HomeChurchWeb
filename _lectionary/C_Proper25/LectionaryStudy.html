Review responses to last week's homework:

Review responses to last week's homework:

  1. Who are the “widows” in our time and place, who are denied justice?

  2. When has our church preferred to focus on its own business, instead of seeking justice for a powerless person or people?

  3. When HAS our church made an effort to seek justice for a powerless person or people?

  4. What does it feel like to “lose heart”?

  5. In what ways are we called to answer the prayers and needs of the powerless?
